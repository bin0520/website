Canvas Usability Assessment for Diverse Students

This remote usability study focused on Guangxi Minzu University's learning management system. It aimed to understand how students—particularly English-language learners—navigate assignments, grades, and messages in Canvas.

  • 📍 Location: China (remote)
  • 👩‍🎓 Participants: 15 undergraduate students
  • 🧪 Method: Task-based testing, survey, timing
